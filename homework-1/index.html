Discover Prokletije & Gusinje

Welcome to my travel blog! Join me as I explore the breathtaking landscapes of Prokletije National Park and the charming town of Gusinje, Montenegro.

About Prokletije National Park

Prokletije, also known as the Accursed Mountains, is one of the most stunning and remote national parks in Montenegro. It offers rugged peaks, pristine valleys, and incredible hiking trails.

Exploring Gusinje

Gusinje is a small town nestled at the foot of Prokletije. It is known for its rich cultural history, stunning waterfalls, and delicious local cuisine.
